About Quran Heaven

Welcome to Quran Heaven, a website designed to facilitate the learning and understanding of the Quran.

Quran Heaven is a free, open-source, and community-driven project, aiming to provide a platform for Muslims and non-Muslims alike to learn and understand the Quran.

The concept of Quran Heaven is to create a space where people can come together to learn and discuss the Quran, share knowledge and ideas, and build a community of like-minded individuals.

The project is built using Django, a Python web framework, and uses a wide range of technologies such as Bootstrap, jQuery, and Font Awesome.

Our mission

Our mission is to make the Quran accessible to everyone, regardless of background or level of experience. We aim to bridge the gap between the timeless wisdom of the Quran and the everyday lives of Muslims and seekers of knowledge around the world. By offering accurate translations, tafsir (exegesis), audio recitations, and interactive learning tools, we hope to foster a deeper connection to the Quran and its teachings.
